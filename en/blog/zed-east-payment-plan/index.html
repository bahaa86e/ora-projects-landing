Frequently asked questions

What is the ZED East payment plan in simple terms?

Typically 5% on contract, another 5% after about 90 days, then installments for up to 10 years on eligible releases—verify every milestone on your ORA SPA.

Where can I read the full ZED East payment plan online?

Start with the dedicated payment plan URL, then request the matrix for your unit from ORA or use the inquiry form for general guidance—release-specific percentages appear on developer paperwork.

Does every ZED East unit qualify for 10-year installments?

Not automatically—length and rhythm depend on inventory band and ORA credit approval at the time of booking.

How do ZED East payment plans differ for townhomes?

Townhomes may carry different deposit curves versus tower stock even within the same masterplan; compare on the official sheet for your typology.

Is a blog enough to book ZED East?

No—use this article for education, then move to consultation or WhatsApp for countersigned ORA documentation.
