Silversands North Coast Prices Egypt

Silversands North Coast prices in Egypt sit at the top of ORA’s Mediterranean pipeline—buyers aren’t hunting “cheap summer stock”; they’re allocating to crystal-lagoon and beach inventory with disciplined quarterly cash calls.

Marketing bands commonly quote roughly 27,000,000 to 128,000,000 EGP depending on chalet vs villa typology, lagoon vs direct sea orientation, and interior scale. Entry is usually a 5% booking on ORA SPAs followed by equal quarterly installments toward handover milestones.
