What investors ask before wiring

“Is Solana East an end-user or yield play?” Both—mature road grids support rentals, but underwriting must include service charges, furnishing cycles, and delivery timing.

“How liquid is resale?” Liquidity follows branded ORA compounds with clear HOAs; still plan a 12–24 month horizon if you need exit optionality.

“What beats spreadsheets?” Countersigned payment matrices and SPA clauses—not broker WhatsApp forwards.
